App Development

GrinSync

In Spring 2024, I worked with a team of 6 to develop a mobile app called GrinSync. This app is designed to help users find and join events on Grinnell College's campus. It allows users to create and join events, and to view event details. Through this app, we hope to help students find events to attend, and to help event organizers reach more people.

I worked on the front-end of the app, using Flutter and Dart. You can find more information about the app on our website and GitHub page.
